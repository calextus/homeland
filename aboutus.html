About Us

HomeLand is an epic, action packed afrocentric comic that journeys through time blending mythologies, fierce tribal warfare and the transformative clash of cultures. Set in a world where ancient afro tribes rise, fall and evolve, this story unveils the ultimate struggle for power, survival and identity as each defend their right to Homeland

From deeply rooted ancient traditions of civilization, to the chaos of colonial encounters and the dawn of a futuristic world, the narritive captures the triumphs, tragedies and resilience of nations forging destiny across time in a world defined by conflict. Experience a saga of power, mysticism and evolution as the past and future colide in a breathtaking new Era of storytelling!
